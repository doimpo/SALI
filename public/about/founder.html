Biography

Prof. Dr. Tom Cherian is a world-renowned expert in liver transplantation and hepatobiliary surgery. With a career spanning over two decades, he has performed more than 2,000 liver transplants and has been instrumental in advancing the field of liver care in South Asia.

He completed his medical education at the prestigious Christian Medical College, Vellore, and pursued further specialization in the United Kingdom. His extensive training and research have led to numerous publications in peer-reviewed journals and have earned him international recognition for his contributions to hepatology.

As a co-founder of the South Asian Liver Institute (SALi), Prof. Dr. Cherian is dedicated to providing affordable, high-quality liver care to patients across the region. His vision is to establish SALi as a center of excellence for liver treatment, research, and education.
